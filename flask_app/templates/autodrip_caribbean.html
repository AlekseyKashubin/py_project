Coffee Recipe


Recipe for Coffee on a Auto Drip Coffee Maker:

1: Add water to the reservoir. Always start with fresh, cold water and measure the correct amount using the markers on the drip brewer.
2: Place the filter into the basket Make sure the coffee filter is the right shape and size for your drip brewer.
3: Add medium ground coffee, Use the 1:18 ratio to calculate how much coffee you need (For a 8-cups .18 x 1892g = 340g).
4: Start the brew your coffee is done once the coffee is no longer dripping; interrupting the brew can affect the flavor of the whole pot.
5: Pour a cup & enjoy your coffee at home for the best taste, serve within 20 minutes or keep your coffee warm in an insulated container.
