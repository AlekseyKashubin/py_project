Coffee Recipe

Water Temp : 205f

Coffee Amount : 24g

Water Amount : 390g

Brew Time : 3 min 30 sec

Grind Size

Recipe for African Coffee On Kalita:

1: Put a filter in the Kalita Wave, and place on top of a server or mug.
2: Fill pouring kettle with hot water and rinse filter until you feel heat on the outside of the cone and discard water.
3: Weigh coffee to (24 grams) and grind.
4: Place the Kalita Wave with server on a scale. Add coffee, shake to level the coffee bed, zero the scale, and fill the pouring kettle with hot water.
5: Start timer and your pour at the same time.
6: Pouring is split into six separate pours of 65g of water in 15 seconds*, and allowing to drain for 15 seconds in between each pour.
7: Start each pour in the center. Work your way out.
8: Coffee should finish draining around 3:30. Remove the Kalita Wave. Serve.
