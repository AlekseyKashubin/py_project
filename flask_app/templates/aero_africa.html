Grind Size

Recipe for African Coffee On Areopress:

1: Put a filter in the Areopress, and place on top of a server or mug and rinse with hot water.
2: Add coffee to chamber and level out the grounds by giving it a shake.
3: Start timer, add 241 grams of water
4: Stir for 10 seconds
5: Place plunger into the chamber and pull up slightly to create vacuum
6: Let sit until 1:45, then plunge for 30 seconds
8: Serve & Enjoy.
