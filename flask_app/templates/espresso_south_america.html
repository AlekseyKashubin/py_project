Coffee Recipe


Recipe for South American Coffee on Espresso:

1: To ensure your dose is accurate, tare the scale with the portafilter on top, grind it into the basket, and then place the portafilter back on the scale.
2: Once you have the right weight, evenly tamp your espresso with 30 lbs of pressure.
3: Grab a clean shot glass or mini metal pitcher, place it on the scale, and zero it out.
4: Place the scale and the glass under the group head, and begin brewing your espresso. Immediately start your timer.
5: At the 29-second mark, stop the shot and read the yield on the scale. If the yield isn’t 42 grams, make some changes and start over. If the yield is below 42 grams, make your grind coarser. If it’s above 42 grams, make your grind finer.
6: -Enjoy!
