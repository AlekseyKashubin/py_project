Recipe for South American Coffee on Espresso

Water Temp : 201f
Dose Amount : 20g
Yield Amount : 42g
Shot time: 29 Seconds
Ratio : 1:2.1

Step One

To ensure your dose is accurate, tare the scale with the portafilter on top, grind it into the basket, and then place the portafilter back on the scale.

Step Two

Once you have the right weight, evenly tamp your espresso with 30 lbs of pressure.

Step Three

Grab a clean shot glass or mini metal pitcher, place it on the scale, and zero it out.

Step Four

Place the scale and the glass under the group head, and begin brewing your espresso. Immediately start your timer.

Step Five

At the 29-second mark, stop the shot and read the yield on the scale. If the yield is below 42 grams, make your grind coarser. If it’s above 42 grams, make your grind finer.

Step Six

-Enjoy
