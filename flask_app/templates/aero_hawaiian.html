Coffee Recipe

Water Temp : 200f

Coffee Amount : 17g

Water Amount : 230

Brew Time : 1:39 min

Grind Size

Recipe for Hawaiian Coffee On Areopress:

1: Put a filter in the Areopress, and place on top of a server or mug and rinse with hot water.
2: Add coffee to chamber and level out the grounds by giving it a shake.
3: Add the 230g of water to the coffee, and cap the top with the plunger to seal it and prevent dripping.
4: Wait 1:00, and then remove the plunger, give it a few stirs, then press it out slowly.
5: Total brew time 1:35 - 1:45
6: Serve & Enjoy.
