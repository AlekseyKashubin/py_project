Proper Bloom

Water Temp : 203f

Coffee Amount : 22g

Water Amount : 360g

Brew Time : 2:30 min

Grind Size

Coffee Recipe


Recipe for Caribbean Coffee On V60:

1: Start by filling up your kettle and setting it to boil.
2: Rinse the paper filter with the now boiled water, ensuring that it is fully saturated.
3: Grind your 22g of coffee to a medium consistency.
4: Position your V60 coffee dripper on top of your coffee server. Ensure that the server will hold more than 400g of coffee to avoid a spill.
5: Add 22g of ground coffee to the V60. With your finger, create a small well in the middle of the grounds.
6: Start the timer and add 50g of water to bloom the coffee. Stir vigorously with your spoon or stir stick. This is to ensure all coffee is evenly saturated.
7: Allow 20 seconds for a de-gas and then start pouring slowly into the center. To prevent water from escaping without extracting the coffee, your pour should be slow and your water line should not go far above the coffee line.
8: When you have reached 360 grams of water, do a last stir to get the grounds off the side & do not leave any coffee high and dry!
9: Your pour should take 1 min and 50 second or up to 2 min and you total contact time should be 2:20 to 2:30.
10: Enjoy.
