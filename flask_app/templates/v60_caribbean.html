Recipe for Caribbean Coffee On V60

Water Temp : 203f
Coffee Amount : 22g
Water Amount : 360g
Brew Time : 2:30 min

Step One

Start by filling up your kettle and setting it to 205f.

Step Two

Rinse the paper filter with the now boiled water, ensuring that it is fully saturated.

Step Three

Grind your 22g of coffee to a medium consistency.

Step Four

Add 22g of ground coffee to the V60. With your finger, create a small well in the middle of the grounds.

Step Five

Start the timer and add 50g of water to bloom the coffee. Stir vigorously with your spoon or stir stick. This is to ensure all coffee is evenly saturated.

Step Six

Allow 20 seconds for a de-gas and then start pouring slowly into the center. To prevent water from escaping without extracting the coffee, your pour should be slow and your water line should not go far above the coffee line.

Step Seven

When you have reached 360 grams of water, do a last stir to get the grounds off the side & do not leave any coffee high and dry!

Step Eight

Your pour should take 1 min and 50 second or up to 2 min and you total contact time should be 2:20 to 2:30.

Step Nine

Serve and Enjoy!
