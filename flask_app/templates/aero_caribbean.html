Recipe for Caribbean Coffee On Areopress

Water Temp : 205f
Coffee Amount : 21g
Water Amount : 300g
Brew Time : 2:30 min

Step One

Put a filter in the Areopress, and place on top of a server or mug and rinse with hot water.

Step Two

Add coffee to chamber and level out the grounds by giving it a shake.

Step Three

Start timer, add 300 grams of water.

Step Four

Stir for 10 seconds

Step Five

Place plunger into the chamber and pull up slightly to create vacuum

Step Six

Let sit until 1:50, then plunge for 30 seconds Serve and Enjoy
