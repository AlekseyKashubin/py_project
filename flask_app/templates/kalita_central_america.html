Recipe for Central American Coffee On a Kalita

Water Temp : 205f
Coffee Amount : 20g
Water Amount : 300g
Brew Time : 3 min

Step One

Put a filter in the Kalita Wave, and place on top of a server or mug. Rinse filter and discard water.

Step Two

Weigh coffee to (20 grams) and grind.

Step Three

Place the Kalita Wave with server on a scale. Add coffee, shake to level the coffee bed, zero the scale, and fill the pouring kettle with hot water.

Step Four

Start timer and your pour at the same time.Pouring is split into six separate pours of 65g of water in 15 seconds*, and allowing to drain for 15 seconds in between each pour.

Step Five

Start each pour in the center. Work your way out.

Step Six

Coffee should finish draining around 3 min mark. Remove the Kalita Wave.

Step Seven

Serve and Enjoy
