Recipe for Central American Coffee On Auto Drip Coffee Maker

Coffee Amount : 97g
Water Amount : 1750g
Coffee Ratio : 1:18

Step One

Add water to the reservoir. Always start with fresh, cold water and measure the correct amount using the markers on the drip brewer.

Step Two

Weigh out 97g of coffee and grind it Medium-Fine.

Step Three

Place the filter into the basket Make sure the coffee filter is the right shape and size for your drip brewer.

Step Four

Add ground coffee, Use the 1:18 ratio to calculate how much coffee you need
(For 7-cups(250g X 7) = 1750g
1750g / 18 Ratio = 97g).

Step Five

Start the brew, your coffee is done once the coffee is no longer dripping. Interrupting the brew can affect the flavor of the whole pot.

Step Six

Pour a cup & enjoy your coffee at home for the best taste, serve within 20 minutes or keep your coffee warm in an insulated container.
